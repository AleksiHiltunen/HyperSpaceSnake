How to Play

Out of nowhere, Snake appears in a very strange place. What place is this.. It cannot be! HYPER SPACE!!!

Hyperspace is very dangerous place, where the attempt to survice is a must. Help Snake to survive for as long as possible by feeding him/her. Mysterious food appears in the Hyperspace from time to time. There seems to be a Hyperloop where Snake is able to hang out.

To control Snake, use the arrow keys or WASD

Avoid colliding the walls of the Hyperspace or yourself, it has sever consequences...

Good Luck!
